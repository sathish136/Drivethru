Policy to Implement
1. REGULAR SHIFT
Standard time: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Late after: 8:15 AM
OT after: 5:30 PM
OT should count only after 30 minutes threshold
2. RECEPTION SHIFT
Standard time: 8:30 AM - 5:30 PM
Late after: 8:45 AM
OT after: 6:00 PM
OT should count only after 30 minutes threshold
3. KITCHEN SHIFT
Shift timing may vary by assigned shift row
Late after: 15 minutes from assigned shift start
OT rule must be based on assigned kitchen shift
If policy says extended OT applies after long shift completion, calculate only from the assigned shift rule, not generic regular shift logic
Do not use random OT decimals without rule basis
4. FLEXIBLE SHIFT
No late deduction
OT only if total worked hours exceed 9 hours 30 minutes
If total worked time is <= 9:30, OT = 0
5. NIGHT SHIFT
Standard shift: 8:00 PM - 5:00 AM
Actual off time can be considered until 8:00 AM
Maximum OT = 3 hours
Hourly punch validation is mandatory
Night Shift OT Deduction Logic

Check hourly punch presence across night blocks.

Expected hourly continuity across the shift window.

Use this rule:

0 missed hourly blocks → full 3 hrs OT
1 missed hourly block → deduct 1 hr OT
2 or more missed hourly blocks → deduct 2 hrs OT
Never deduct more than 2 hrs

Night shift remarks must clearly show:

Night Shift - 3 hrs OT added
Night Shift - 1 hr OT deducted due to missed punch
Night Shift - 2 hrs OT deducted due to multiple missed punches
Common Rules
Late Grace
General grace = 15 minutes
After grace only, calculate actual late minutes

Examples:

Shift start 8:00, punch 8:10 → no late
Shift start 8:00, punch 8:18 → late = 3 mins
OT Threshold
OT should only start counting after 30 minutes
Example:
Shift end 5:00
OT start threshold 5:30
Punch out 5:45
OT = 15 mins
Grace Adjustments

Apply before final late/OT result:

Lunch grace = +10 mins
Final checkout grace = +5 mins

If these are used, remarks must mention them.
