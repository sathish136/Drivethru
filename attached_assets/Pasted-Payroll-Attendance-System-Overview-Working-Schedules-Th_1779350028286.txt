Payroll & Attendance System Overview
Working Schedules
The company operates under two different working schedules:
Main Season
Includes overtime calculations, late hour deductions, lunch incentive payments, and shift systems according to
employment category.
Off Season
Excludes overtime, late hour deductions, and shift systems. During off season, employees are only required to
maintain normal attendance through sign-ins and sign-outs in the attendance system.
General Payroll Rules
Lunch Incentive
- Paid at Rs. 125 per working day
- Also applicable for approved half days
Attendance
- All employees must record attendance through the LiveU Attendance Cloud System.
- Attendance calculations are based on recorded punches.
Kitchen Department
Main Season
Monday to Friday: 7:00 AM – 9:00 PM (including 2-hour lunch break)
Saturday: 8:00 AM – 2:00 PM (half day without lunch break)
Sunday: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM (including 1-hour lunch break)
Off Season
- No fixed shift system
- Normal attendance with sign-ins and sign-outs only
- No overtime
- No late hour deductions
Room Attendants, Gardeners, Accounting & Management
Main Season
Working Day: 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM (including 1-hour lunch break)
Half Day: 8:00 AM – 2:00 PM (without lunch break)
Off Season
- No fixed shift system
- Normal attendance with sign-ins and sign-outs only
- No overtime
- No late hour deductions
Reception Department
Main Season
Working Day: 8:30 AM – 5:30 PM (including 1-hour lunch break)
Half Day: 8:30 AM – 2:30 PM (without lunch break)
Off Season
- No fixed shift system
- Normal attendance with sign-ins and sign-outs only
- No overtime
- No late hour deductions
Surf Instructors
Main Season
- No fixed working schedule
- Working hours depend on surf lesson bookings and guest schedules
- Full working day: 9 hours including 1-hour lunch break
- Half day: 5 hours without lunch break
- No late hour deductions
Attendance must reflect all activity periods through punch records in the LiveU Attendance Cloud System.
Example of Punch Sequence
1. Lesson start – IN
2. Lesson finish and leaving after lesson – OUT
3. Return for theory session – IN
4. Sign OUT for lunch break
5. Sign IN after lunch break
6. Going for second lesson and final sign OUT after completing work and going home
Off Season
- No fixed shift system
- Normal attendance with sign-ins and sign-outs only
- No overtime
- No late hour deductions
Night Watchers
All Year Round
- Duty schedule: Every second day
- Shift timing: 8:00 PM – 8:00 AM
- 12-hour shift including 3 hours overtime
- No lunch break deduction
- Late hour deductions apply
Previously, hourly punch records were used to confirm active duty performance and prevent sleeping during shifts.
In case an alternative monitoring method is implemented for duty performance verification, access to 12 punch
records in the LiveU Attendance Cloud System will no longer be required.
